stone / cupping / sauna sold as +$30 add-ons, Prenatal added as a standard service, and
foot-reflexology + head-spa exclusive to Elite (J Massage refers those to Elite as a sister
location).

- Each repo: talech WIP committed first, then a **separate pricing commit**, on its existing branch. Nothing pushed or deployed.
