FAQ

Pricing Questions

How much does a massage cost at J Massage SLC?
Massages start at $55 for a 30-minute Swedish or deep tissue session. A 60-minute Swedish is $90, deep tissue or sports massage is $95, Ashiatsu is $100. Couples massage starts at $170 for 60 minutes and 4-hand massage starts at $165. All prices are listed in full above — no hidden fees.
Is there a fee for paying by card?
A small card processing fee may apply when paying by credit or debit card. There is no additional fee when paying with cash. This is confirmed at the time of booking so there are no surprises.
Can I add the infrared sauna to my massage session?
Yes. The infrared sauna is available as a $35 add-on to any massage session — just select it when booking online or mention it when you call. You can also book the sauna as a standalone 30-minute session for $45, subject to availability.
Are gift cards available?
Yes — J Massage SLC gift cards are available for any dollar amount or for a specific service. They never expire and make a thoughtful gift for any occasion. Visit our gift cards page or call (801) 288-1118 to purchase.
